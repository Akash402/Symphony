Parvaah-A Symphony Initiative

Parvaah started off with an iron will to make a difference. To start off somewhere, Parvaah went to Mahila Atyachar Pratibandh Kaksha ( Prevention of Crimes Against Women Cell ) of the Mumbai Police for hard data on Crimes Against Women. The data did appal us, but it also gave us determination to strive harder for this cause. Our efforts gained the attention of Mrs. Gavit, the A.C.P. of Mahila Atyachar Pratibandh Kaksha . After sitting down and discussing the issue extensively with Mrs. Gavit, we were given a number of suggestions. The detailed discussion was the basis on which we built our action plan.
With the support of Mumbai Police, we approached various organizations for help.
Our cause received the support of Mumbai Taxi menâ€™s Union. After discussing the cause with Mr. Anthony Quadros, the head of the union. We came to the conclusion that to make taxis a safe mode of transport, ladies should be made aware about the safety precautions.
So team PARVAAH decided to make all the taxies equipped with the guidelines.
Our hard work started causing a stir and also gained the attention of the Honourable Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Devendra Fadnavis, who pledged his support to our cause.
